Green steel can have several meanings, but often refers to steel that is produced with hydrogen, produced by clean energy electrolysis, rather than coal as most steel is produced today. Green steel is generally too expensive to produce widely today, except where electricity is cheap and low-carbon. The price of green steel is likely to fall in coming years relative to alternatives [^"6"].
