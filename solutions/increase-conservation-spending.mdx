## Description

We reccomend increased conservation spending.

## Background

As the benefits of conservation greatly outweigh the cost, there is a case to increase public and philanthropic conservation spending.

Current spending on conservation falls short of what is needed to protect all biodiversity.

<ArticleImage image={"cons_finance.svg"} width={750} height={370} />
#### Sources: [^"12"], [^"19"].

Conservation spending, in inflation-adjusted terms, from the U.S. Department of Agriculture has remained flat in recent years, and there is a strong case for increasing that spending [^"15"].

[^"19"]: Global Canopy Program. [*The Little Biodiversity Finance Book.*](https://www.globalcanopy.org/insights/publication/the-little-biodiversity-finance-book-3rd-edition-2012/) 3rd Edition. 2012.
