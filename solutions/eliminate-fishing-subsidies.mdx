export const problem = "Impact of Fishing";

A large portion of fishing on the high seas (outside of any country's exclusive economic zone) is uneconomical, but the amount of such fishing is inflated by subsidies [^"29"]. We propose elimination of the subsidies, both on the high seas and in an EEZ.

<ArticleImage image={"fishing_subsidy.svg"} width={900} height={600} />
#### Source: [^"29"].

Overall, countries gave over $35 billion in fishing subsidies in 2018, including subsidies for fuel consumption and others that encourage overfishing.

<ArticleImage image={"fishing_subsidy2.svg"} width={900} height={600} />
#### Sources: [^"30"], [^"31"].

Negotiations at the World Trade Organization to eliminate subsidies progressed in 2022 and are encouraging, but there are more details to agree upon [^"9"].
