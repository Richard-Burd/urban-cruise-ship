import SolutionDropdown from '/components/SolutionDropdown.js'

export const problem = "Abandoned Mines";
export const solution = "Effective Reclamation Bonds";

Mine operators are required to post bonds for eventual reclamation of the site, but this requirement is not generally adequate [^1]. We propose that bonds be increased to reflect the expected cost of cleanup.

[^1]: Buck, S., Gerard, D. ["Cleaning Up Mining Waste"](https://www.perc.org/wp-content/uploads/old/rs01_1.pdf). Political Economy Research Center, Research Study 01-1. November 2001.
