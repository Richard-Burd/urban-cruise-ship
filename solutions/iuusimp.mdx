Reporting requirements will help reduce the market share for IUU fishing. The United States conducts the Seafood Import Monitoring Program (SIMP) [^{ "39" }], which establishes recordkeeping requirements for almost half of U.S. seafood imports. The program should be expanded to cover all imports and expanded worldwide through treaty agreement.

[^{ 39 } ]: NOAA Fisheries ["Seafood Import Monitoring Program"](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/international/seafood-import-monitoring-program). Accessed October 22, 2022.
