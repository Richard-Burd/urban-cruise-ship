Heavy industry creates significant air pollution, damaging humans and ecosystems. Moving this industry to the deep ocean, far from human settlements and biodiversity hotspots, will reduce this damage.

If the demand for oil drops as alternatives gain market share, there may be a surplus of offshore oil rigs. These rigs could be appropriated for industrial or other uses [^{ "7" }], [^{ "8" }], [^{ "10" }]. Retired ships, such as the ocean linear Queen Elizabeth 2, can also be repurposed for more stationary uses [^{ "2" }].
